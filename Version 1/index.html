How to Become a Dragon Master

Explore the world of Dragonomica and see if you have what it takes to become a dragon master. Become versed in the ever expansive knowledge about dragons and how to deal with them and when to leave them alone. Your journey will take you to the far reaches of the world to learn all about dragons and their habits;  to the staggering peaks of the mountains to the murky depths of the oceans to the scorching lands of the desert. Do you have what it takes to become a dragon master?

Start your Journey Here

  • Learn about a world where dragons thrive
  • Discover the history of dragons and how they came about
  • Search the skies and know the difference between dragons and Wyverns
  • Get to know the different species and breeds of dragons and how they live
  • If you dare to go deeper, learn about their biology and anatomy

How to Tame a Dragon

  • Some dragons are capable of being tamed
  • Learn to recognize which dragon breeds you can and cannot tame
  • Know how to keep and preserve your bond​​
  • Learn how to properly care for your dragon
  • Learn to recognize body language and when to give them space
