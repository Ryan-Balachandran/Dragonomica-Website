Why a Website on Dragons?

I created this website to inform others about dragons, to help them understand that not all of them are mean or malicious. When someone asks you what you know about dragons, it will most likely be from how they are depicted in movies. While there is a fair few dragons that are good, they don't go in depth about how they live or their history. I am here to inform you about the more subtle details you may not know about.
